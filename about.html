About Senecal CleanPro Inc.

Our Story

Senecal CleanPro Inc. has been proudly serving Gatineau and the surrounding areas with top-quality cleaning services. Founded with a commitment to excellence, we specialize in commercial, residential, and sanitation cleaning, ensuring every space we touch is spotless and healthy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable, thorough, and environmentally responsible cleaning solutions. We prioritize customer satisfaction and safety, using the latest cleaning techniques and eco-friendly products to protect our clients and the environment.

Our Team

Our team is composed of highly trained, professional cleaners dedicated to delivering outstanding service every time. We continuously invest in training and technology to maintain our position as a trusted leader in the cleaning industry.

  • Customized cleaning plans
  • Use of eco-friendly products
  • Competitive pricing with excellent value
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your needs
